Covid19 Basic Reproductive Ratio
Covid19 R0

The Covid19 virus basic reproductive ratio R0 (pronounced R nought or R zero) represents the number of newly infected individuals directly generated by a single infected individual in the population, where all individuals are susceptible to infection.
An important fact is that R0 is not a biological constant. The reproductive ratio can vary with different environmental conditions and the behavior of the affected population

How do scientists use R0?

R0 is an estimate of the infectious rate of the virus in a population. When R0>1, an infected individual can infect more than 1 person, and thus the number of infections in the population would increase. When R0<1, the epidemy will slowly decrease and resolve.
R0 can also be used to estimate the percentage of the population that needs to be immune to the virus to offer herd immunity and protect the population.
